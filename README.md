# BookHeaven 📚

> Your paradise for books and stationery in Nairobi.

## About
BookHeaven is an online bookshop and stationery store based in Westlands, Nairobi, Kenya. Founded in 2023 by Nathanael Kitambala, a Congolese entrepreneur who expanded his passion for books into the Kenyan market. BookHeaven offers a wide selection of books and stationery products delivered across Nairobi.

## Pages
- Home — Hero section, featured products, and store highlights
- About — Our story, mission, vision, and team
- Products — Filterable product grid (Books & Stationery)
- Product Detail — Individual pages for each book
- Blog — Reading tips and book reviews
- Testimonials — Customer reviews and ratings
- FAQ — Frequently asked questions with accordion
- Contact — Contact form with JavaScript validation

## JavaScript Features
- Product filter — Filter products by category (Books/Stationery)
- Contact form validation — Validates all fields before submission
- Dark/Light mode toggle — Remembers user preference across pages
- Back to top button — Appears on scroll and smoothly returns to top

## Technologies Used
- HTML5
- CSS3
- JavaScript ES6+
- Bootstrap 5
- Bootstrap Icons
