About BookHeaven

Our story, our mission, and the team behind the books.

Our Story

BookHeaven was founded in 2023 by Nathanael Kitambala, a passionate reader who believed that every Kenyan deserved access to quality books and stationery at affordable prices.

What started as a small online store has grown into a trusted bookshop serving customers across Nairobi, with a physical location in Westlands.

Today, BookHeaven stocks over 5,000 book titles and hundreds of stationery products, catering to students, professionals, and passionate readers alike.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To make reading and learning accessible to every Kenyan by providing a wide range of affordable, high-quality books and stationery with excellent customer service.

Our Vision

To become East Africa's most trusted and beloved bookshop, inspiring a culture of reading and lifelong learning across the continent.
